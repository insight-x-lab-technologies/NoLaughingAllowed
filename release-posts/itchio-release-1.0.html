No Laughing Allowed is now live on itch.io

The first full release of No Laughing Allowed is here: a quick party game about reading jokes out loud, keeping a straight face, and turning every round into a tiny comedy showdown.

Play on itch.io See what is included

The straight-face challenge begins

In No Laughing Allowed, one player reads a joke while the other side tries not to laugh. If the delivery breaks them, the reader scores. If everyone holds it together, silence wins the moment.

It is built for fast rounds, loud reactions, and groups that enjoy turning simple jokes into dramatic performances. You can play locally on one screen or use a connected companion device to make the active reader's turn feel more theatrical.

What is included in 1.0

  • Quick matches with automatic rounds, scoring, winners, and final results.
  • Teams mode and 1 vs 1 mode for different group sizes.
  • Joke categories, reading challenges, and configurable timers.
  • Companion-device play for showing the joke or timer on a second screen.
  • Custom jokes, extra content pack support, local joke ratings, and a leaderboard.
  • Language, sound, music, appearance, and installable app-style settings.
